# BH-AI-ML-assignment5.1
This repository contains an exploratory data analysis (EDA) project focused on customer coupon acceptance behavior. The analysis investigates how factors such as customer lifestyle, travel convenience, weather, time of day, and demographics influence acceptance and rejection of coupons using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.

---------------------------
Link to the notebook [coupon_analysis.ipynb](https://github.com/RaniDeepLearning/BH-AI-ML-assignment5.1/blob/main/coupon_analysis.ipynb)

---------------------------
# Objectives

The goals of this analysis were to:

 .Explore patterns influencing coupon acceptance and rejection.
 .Identify characteristics of customers more likely to accept bar and coffee coupons.
 .Investigate how weather, travel direction, and convenience affect coupon behavior.
 .Analyze coffee house visiting behavior across demographic groups.
 .Generate actionable insights from customer behavior trends.

-----------------------------------


# Key Findings
-----------Bar Coupon Analysis---------------

 .Drivers who frequently visited bars were much more likely to accept bar coupons.
 .Younger drivers and socially active customers showed higher bar coupon acceptance rates.
 .Drivers traveling without children were more likely to accept bar coupons.
 .Frequent bar visitors with adult passengers showed some of the highest acceptance rates.

------------Coffee Coupon Analysis--------------

. Customers who already visit coffee houses regularly were much more likely to accept coffee coupons.
. Coffee coupon acceptance was highest during morning and early afternoon hours.
.Drivers were less likely to accept coupons requiring additional travel time or opposite-direction travel.
.Students and people working in computer and mathematical occupations appeared to visit coffee houses most frequently.
.Gender showed very little difference in frequent coffee house visiting behavior.


-------------------------------------------------

# Actionable Insights

 .Bar and coffee coupons may work best when targeted toward customers who already visit those locations regularly.
 .Morning and early afternoon promotions may improve coffee coupon effectiveness.
 .Convenience appears to strongly influence coupon acceptance, especially for travel-heavy offers.
 .Occupation and lifestyle patterns may provide stronger marketing signals than gender alone.
 .Businesses may improve coupon performance by targeting behavior-based customer groups instead of broad promotions.
